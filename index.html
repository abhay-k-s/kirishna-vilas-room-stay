Nearby Attractions

Explore Kerala

Legendary temples, museums, and beaches — all within easy reach

Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple

One of the 108 Divya Desam shrines dedicated to Lord Vishnu, famed for its stunning Dravidian architecture and immense historical significance.

~2 km

Attukal Bhagavathy Temple

The "Sabarimala of Women," home to the world's largest gathering of women devotees — a UNESCO record-holding cultural marvel.

~3 km

Pazhavangadi Ganapathi Temple

A beloved Ganesha temple renowned for its unique rituals and devotion, drawing thousands of pilgrims from across Kerala every day.

~3 km

Kuthira Malika Palace

The "Horse Palace" — an architectural masterpiece of traditional Kerala craftsmanship with 122 wooden horses adorning its corridors.

~3 km

Napier Museum

A Gothic–Indo-Saracenic architectural gem housing priceless bronze idols, ancient ornaments, ivories, and a fascinating natural history collection.

~5 km

Shanghumugham Beach

A serene city beach perfect for evening walks, featuring the iconic mermaid sculpture by artist Kanayi Kunhiraman and spectacular sunsets.

~6 km

Kovalam Beach

Kerala's most celebrated beach destination — three crescent-shaped bays with golden sands, lighthouse views, and world-class Ayurvedic resorts.

~16 km
